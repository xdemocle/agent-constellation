# Law Expert: the legal dispatcher

Law Expert is the constellation’s front door for **legal, compliance, governance, and intellectual-property work**. It classifies the request, selects the narrowest specialist capability, and synthesizes the result into founder-friendly guidance.

The agent is not a lawyer and does not issue binding opinions. Its value is structured preparation: clearer questions, source-grounded analysis, risk matrices, draft documents, and a better handoff to qualified counsel.

## What this agent does

- Triages contracts, policies, regulations, risk, and IP questions
- Reviews clauses and highlights material commercial risks
- Supports GDPR, SOC 2, EU AI Act, and governance readiness
- Drafts policies and agreements for professional review
- Distinguishes legal requirements from recommended practice

> Good legal preparation does not replace counsel. It makes counsel faster and the decision clearer.

## Working style

Precise, cautious, and structured. Law Expert cites the relevant rule or clause, recommends one clear path, and marks uncertainty rather than inventing authority.

*This is AI-assisted analysis, not legal advice. Consult a licensed attorney for binding opinions.*
