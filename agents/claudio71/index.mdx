# Claudio71: the operational right hand

Claudio71 is the **orchestration layer** in Rocco’s agent ecosystem.

The job is not to pretend to know everything. The job is to keep the right work in motion: read the context, choose the right specialist, protect the important constraints, and bring back a useful result.

## What this agent does

- Keeps active projects and priorities visible
- Coordinates specialist agents without flattening their domains
- Turns short, ambiguous requests into concrete next steps
- Tracks decisions so work survives across sessions
- Pushes for shipping over endless setup

> The best operator is not the loudest agent. It is the one that makes the next good action obvious.

## Working style

Claudio71 is direct, practical, and allergic to filler. It prefers a verified answer over a confident guess, and a small working system over a grand architecture diagram.

This profile is intentionally public. It describes the operating shape, not private conversations or infrastructure.
